Focus Correction

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

Summary

Focus Correction is a focus-tracking app. We respect your privacy, and this policy explains how we handle your data. In short: your focus data stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information automatically.

Data We Collect

This app does not collect any personal data automatically. Everything you create in the app — including your nickname, focus sessions, and distraction statistics — is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. None of this data is sent to any external server.

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored in your device's app sandbox and protected by the operating system's security mechanisms. Uninstalling the app permanently removes all associated data.

Third-Party Services

The app's core focus-tracking functionality works entirely offline. We use a small number of third-party services strictly for the features you choose to engage:

Google Play Billing — when you purchase the lifetime unlock, the transaction is processed by Google Play. Your payment details (credit card, billing address) go directly to Google and are never seen by this app. We only receive a non-sensitive purchase confirmation token used to unlock the app on your device.

Formspree (formspree.io) — delivers feedback messages you voluntarily send through the in-app contact form. When you submit feedback, the message text and any contact information you choose to include are forwarded to the developer via Formspree. Submitting feedback is entirely optional.

We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Children's Privacy

This app is not designed for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Policy Changes

If we update this Privacy Policy, you will be notified through an app update. Significant changes will be displayed in the app.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the contact form inside the app (Settings → Feedback).
